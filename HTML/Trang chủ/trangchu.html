Provide full service

We provide all necessary equipment for the trip such as backpacks, hats, climbing sticks, meals, transportation..

Professional team

Viettrekking has a team tour guide professionally with many years of experience, trained in survival skills, first aid qualifications.

Separate team of logistics and chefs

We have a team of local porters to support each tour with a 1:1 ratio and professional chefs to prepare food from savory to vegetarian for each group upon request.

Small group tour

Our climbing tours are usually only maximum 12 people/group, while ensuring the connection and safety for the trip, helping customers to ensure their health, and at the same time building a space for private entertainment and discovery. reduce the risks associated with the disease.

VIETTREKKING SAPA HOTEL

Viettrekking Sapa is located on Hoang Lien street, with only 5 minutes walking distance to Stone Church and Sapa center. Possessing a beautiful view with the panoramic view of Fansipan mountain range and Muong Hoa valley, Viettrekking Sapa will bring an ideal perspective during your trip to Sapa. Our hotel is located just enough to avoid the hustle and bustle in the center of Sapa, giving you moments of relaxation and comfort during your stay.

In addition, you can also enjoy the great food of the restaurant, and use the infinity pool on the top floor in the hotel. To increase the interesting experience, we have built gentle trekking tours to village locations in Sa Pa. Finally, our friendly and knowledgeable team will ensure an unforgettable stay with you.
