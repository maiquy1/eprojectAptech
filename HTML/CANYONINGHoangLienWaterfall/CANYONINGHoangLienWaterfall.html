NOTES WHEN JOINING

The individual does not have diseases of blood pressure, calcium deficiency, asthma, cardiovascular disease, epilepsy or fear of heights

(In case you have a history of cardiovascular disease, high blood pressure, osteoarthritis, disc herniation, fear of heights, …contact us for more advice.)

Vegetarian and allergic to foods

(In case of vegetarianism or allergies to any food, please contact a consultant for the best support)

Trim fingernails and toenails before participating

(Avoid injury during climbing and total toe injury caused by shoes and foreign objects when walking on the road)

REASONS TO CHOOSE TQNV

Provide full service

We provide all necessary equipment for the trip such as backpacks, hats, climbing sticks, meals, transportation…

Professional team

TQNV has a team of professional guides with many years of experience, trained in survival skills, first aid degrees with international degrees in Canyoning.

A unit specializing in experiential tours

Viettrekking is a unit specializing in organizing climbing tours as well as experience tours with more than 8 years of experience. We believe in giving you the best experience in every trip.

Separate kitchen and logistics team

We have a team of local porters to support each tour with a 1:1 ratio and professional chefs to prepare food from savory to vegetarian for each group upon request.
